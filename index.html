About

I was born and raised in Orange, California with a passion for travel and adventure. After living in Texas, Oklahoma, and then Melbourne, Australia, I have settled (for the time being!) in New York City. I worked in foodservice until I hung up my apron and ventured into tech - it's been a wild ride so far, and I don't plan on stopping any time soon. In my free time, I'm usually with friends, playing dodgeball, video or board games, or at a concert.

Projects

Social Q

Github | Live



Social Q is a website that gives content creators greater visibility and control over their schedules. They can view a calendar of their posts across their social accounts, schedule posts, and view engagement stats.

Built using the MERN stack


EventBite

Github | Live



EventBite is a website where vampires (and Florence and the Machine fans) can come together in a community to find events and large gatherings in order to find their next meal. Producers (who are vampires themselves, organizing events pretending to be regular humans) can put together events (music festivals, parades, etc) to dupe humans into gathering together for the benefit of our vampire brethren. Refunds only given if the event ends up taking place in direct sunlight.

Built using a Rails back end and React-Redux front end.


LGBTQ+ Rights at a Glance

Github | Live



This is a chart showing the status of LGBTQ+ rights throughout the US. My goal is to bring awareness to the challenges (and triumphs) queer people have achieved, and will continue to achieve...
to facilitate open discussions and set the groundwork for further action. All data comes from the HRC.

Built using HTML/CSS/Javascript
